Create a weekly update suitable for a team one on one personal review. Use a concise tone that is direct, factual, and oriented around outcomes, risks, and decisions. Keep the update to one page or less.

Include these sections in order:

1. Summary of the week in 3 to 5 sentences, covering outcomes, impact, and priority alignment
2. Key wins with measurable impact, list up to 5 and include metrics where possible
3. Deliverables shipped, or notable milestones reached, include dates and owners
4. Risks and blockers, include severity, mitigation, and needed help
5. Decisions made or pending, include decision owner and timeline
6. Cross team dependencies and escalations, include status and next action
7. Next week focus, list top 3 priorities with expected outcomes
8. Links and references, include tickets, commits, docs, or dashboards

If information is missing, explicitly call it out under the relevant section and recommend what data to collect next week.


Write the update in markdown format to "weekly-update.md"
